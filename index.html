Insight
Returning organic users convert 5.8× higher than new — protect the retention loop.

Returning visitors deliver 80% of organic revenue from 26% of sessions. Lower bounce (27% vs 38%), higher depth (0.0 vs 0.0 pages) and ~4.4× session length. With new-visitor CVR at just 0.3%, the priority is converting first-time organic traffic: faster PDPs, clearer sizing, and a first-order incentive.
